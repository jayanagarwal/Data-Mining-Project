Job Overview And Responsibility
You will be responsible for the design of user interfaces and experiences for our digital products. You will work with other UI/UX designers and multiple cross functional teams, including product managers, developers, and stakeholders, to create intuitive and user centered designs that elevate our products. Key Responsibilities:
User Centered Design:
Translate high level requirements into well considered and designed wireframes, and translating wireframes into highly intuitive and compelling user experiences
Create wireframes, prototypes, and user flows to communicate design concepts.
Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing to gather insights and understand user needs. User Interface Design:
Develop and maintain a consistent visual design language, systems and frameworks
Create high quality UI designs and high fidelity prototypes
Design user interfaces that align with product design language and project objectives.
Collaborate with developers to ensure the implementation of designs meets design specifications. Interaction Design:
Define and design interactions and animations that enhance the user experience.
Ensure seamless navigation and intuitive user journeys throughout the digital product. Cross Functional Collaboration:
Collaborate closely with product managers, developers, and other stakeholders to understand project requirements and constraints and develop innovative solutions for product direction and experiences
Advocate for user centric design principles and provide design guidance during the development process. Prototyping and Testing:
Create interactive prototypes to demonstrate design concepts and user flows.
Conduct usability testing and gather feedback to iterate and refine designs. Design Documentation:
Prepare design documentation, including design specifications, style guides, and design assets for development teams.
Maintain organized design files and version control. Required Skills and Experience
Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, User Experience Design, or a related field.
2+ years of proven experience as a UI/UX Designer with a strong portfolio, preferably with showcasing work related to software for productivity.
Proficiency in design and prototyping tools such as Figma, Sketch, or similar.
Strong understanding of user centered design principles and usability best practices.
Excellent visual design skills with an eye for detail and aesthetics.
